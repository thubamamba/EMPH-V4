About us

E & M Plant and Transport specialises in the hiring of Roll-back trucks and Crane Trucks as well as lowbeds. We have been rendering our services to the community for the past 12 years. We pride ourselves in good quality service, quality branded machinery and highly trained staff.

Our aim is to establish and maintain long term relationships with our clients by providing a service that is measurable and cost-effective with the client's best interest in mind at all times.

Why Choose Us

Affordable

Not only do we aspire for great service; but we provide inexpensive, market related rates.

High quality machinery

All our trucks are fitted with quality equipment and machinery that is well maintained and certified by professionals. We have an in house workshop that keeps our Cranes and Trucks serviced, which reduces the risk of breakdowns.

Dependable

No job is too big or too small. E&M Plant and Transport is always willing to go the extra mile for customer satisfaction. We find that communication and authenticity is the key to success and reliability.
